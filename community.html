Community Involvement

Charity and Community Giving

Over the years, the bookstore has donated hundreds of books to local shelters, literacy programs, and underfunded schools through seasonal drives and year-round initiatives.

Partnering with Local Schools

Book Haven Bookstore is proud to collaborate with local schools to support student learning and foster a love for reading from an early age. Through organized school visits, reading list discounts, and educational workshops, the bookstore provides teachers and students with the resources they need to thrive.

Events and Collaborations

We’re excited to announce our annual Children’s Book Festival, this family-friendly event will feature live readings by beloved children’s authors, arts and crafts stations, a book donation drive for local schools, and free storybooks for the first 100 children. It’s part of our ongoing effort to make reading fun, accessible, and meaningful for the next generation of readers.
